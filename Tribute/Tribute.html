M. Shadows

Singer and Songwriter
M. Shadows, like the other members of Avenged Sevenfold, uses a stage name. In an interview, Shadows says that he chose his stage name because he thought of himself as
"The darker character in the group".

    Time line of M. Shadows:

  • 1981 - Born on Fountain, CA
  • 1999 - M. Shadows formed 'Avenged Sevenfold', a heavy metal band, whose current lineup comprises Shadows as the lead vocalist, Zacky Vengeance as the rhythm guitarist and backing vocalist, Synyster Gates as the lead guitarist and backing vocalist, Johnny Christ, the bassist and backing vocalist, and drummer Brooks Wackerman.
  • 2003 - When Shadows was in the 'Successful Failure' band, he had written the song 'Streets', which was later used in 'Avenged Sevenfold's' second studio album, 'Waking The Fallen'
  • 2005 - Their third album and first major label release was 'City of Evil'
  • 2007 - M. Shadows was featured on the Steel Panther album 'Feel the Steel', and also produced The Confession's album, 'Requiem'
  • 2010 - Despite the death of drummer James 'The Rev' Sullivan in 2009, the band continued with the new drummer Mike Portnoy and released their fifth album 'Nightmare' on July 27, which debuted at No. 1 on the Billboard 200. Shadows has also made several guest appearances on albums by other artists like 'Slash'.
  • 2013 - The band's sixth studio album 'Hail to the King' was released on August 23, The album charted at No. 1 on the Billboard 200 and also on UK, Brazilian, Finnish, Canadian, and Irish charts.
  • 2016 - Avenged Sevenfold's seventh studio album 'The Stage' was released on October 28, which debuted at No. 4 on Billboard 200 chart. This album marked a stylistic change in the band's music, which moved towards more progressive metal sound.
